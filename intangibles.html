About Intangibles
“Intangibles” can be divided into 3 main types.
Intellectual Property (IP)

Non-physical assets with special legal rights which protect your business model and you can use to stop copying.

Examples include: Registered patents, trademarks, designs and unregistered copyrights and trade secrets.

Intellectual Assets

Identifiable assets that you can legally own, use, trade and/or license.

Examples include: Contracts, permits, data, relationships, awards

Intellectual Capital

Assets you don’t “own”, but benefit from using.

Examples include: Skills and experience of your team

IPExplorer only focuses on Indonesian IP; IP needs to be protected or registered in other countries before you trade abroad.
